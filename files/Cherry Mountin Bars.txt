Grandma Chris's Cherry Mountain Bars

Cherry layer:
1/2 butter
2 cups sugar
2/3 cup evaporated milk
12 large marshmallows
5 t Watkins cherry flavoring
6oz White chocolate chips
1 t vanilla
Red food coloring


Chocolate layer:
1/2 cup butter
3/4 chopped peanuts (cocktail preferred)
1 t vanilla
12oz milk chocolate chips
3/4 cup peanut butter


Grease a 9x13 pan with butter.

For the first layer:
Combine butter, sugar, evap milk, and marshmallows in a medium sauce pan. Heat to gentle boil for 5 mins. Remove from
heat add white choc chips, mix until melted add a few drops of red food coloring. Mix in cherry flavoring and vanilla.
Pour into Refrigerate until set.

For the second layer:
In a medium sauce pan (or double boiler) melt the butter. Add chocolate, peanut butter and vanilla, stir constantly
until melted and combined. Stir in peanuts. Pour on cherry layer and refrigerate until set.
