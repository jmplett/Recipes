Peanut Butter Balls

Ingredients:
    - 3 1/2 C. Rice Krispies
    - 2 1/4 C. Peanut Butter
    - 2 1/2 C. Powdered Sugar
    - Chocolate Chips for Dipping

Directions:
    Mix first 4 ingredients. (Hands work best.) Roll into balls the size of walnuts. Dip in the melted chocolate
    coating. Cool and let harden on wax paper. These freeze well.
